⚠️ Delete Account

You can permanently delete your Moalimee account through the app.

⚠️
Important Warning: When you delete your account, all your data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

Data that will be deleted:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone)
  • Your progress history in lessons and quizzes
  • Your points, achievements, and level
  • All your account data

Steps to delete your account:

  1. Open the Moalimee app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Enter your password for confirmation
  5. Tap Delete My Account Permanently
